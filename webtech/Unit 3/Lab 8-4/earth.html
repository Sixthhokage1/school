Earth is the planet that we live on. It is the third planet from the Sun and as far as we know, the only plant that has any form of life in the entire Solar System. The atmosphere consists primarily of nitrogen, oxygen, argon, carbon dioxide. The gravity of the planet holds the atmosphere to the planet giving life to all that inhabit the planet.
