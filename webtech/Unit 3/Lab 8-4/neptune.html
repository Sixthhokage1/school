Neptune is the last of the gas giants. The planet is primarily made up of hydrogen, helium, methane, traces of ammonia and water. It is believed it has a rocky core as well. Neptune has a ring system but it is made up primarily of dust particles. Neptune's orbit crosses that of Pluto making it sometimes the farthest planet from the sun.
