Uranus is a gas giant that consists primarily of water, ammonia, and methane ice. It is believed that there is a rocky core at the center of the planet. Uranus is the only planet that rotates on its side believed to the result of the planet being hit by a large object at one point. Uranus also has a ring system, but it is dark and very small, only a few miles wide making it very difficult to see.
