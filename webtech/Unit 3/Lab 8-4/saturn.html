Saturn most distinctive feature is its ring system with particles ranging in size from dust to as large as a building. The age of the rings are not known exactly. The width of the rings are so large that the main ring would barely fit between the Earth and its Moon. Saturn is primarily a huge ball of hydrogen and a small percentage of helium with a small rocky core in the center.
