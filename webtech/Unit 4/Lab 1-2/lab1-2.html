Ada Lovelace

Known as the Father of Computing. Designed the Analytical Engine and built a prototype for a less powerful mechanical calculator. Invented and developed but never completed the Difference Engine.

Founded Micro Instrumentation and Telemetry Systems and developed the first personal computer, Altair 8800 and became known as the Father of Personal Computers.

The first computer programmer. Ada wrote the first algorithm processed by a machine. She also assisted Charles Babbage with the development of the Difference Engine.
